Our Work

Educate

Here at Bits & Skills, we prioritise learning and skill acquisition. We believe that a candle does not go off by lighting another hence our teachers are dedicated to ensuring our learners get the most of their time together.

Empower

By giving our learners a platform to showcase their learnt skills and talents, we promote courage and self-esteem in their work. They get the power to use their skills to earn a living and also educate others.

Elevate

Our vision is to have a hub where the creatives from Kibra and many other slums can come together and share ideas, showcase their ideas, collaborate and be free through creating a sense of belonging for our learners.
